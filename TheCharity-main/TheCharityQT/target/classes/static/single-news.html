Love in the Frontier

The Love in the Frontier project will have a three-year cycle, establishing continuous paired assistance relationships with counties and schools. The invited excellent schools in the city and the border project schools will conduct regular online exchanges and offline visits, as well as various activities such as teaching and research lectures, same class heterogeneity, listening guidance, and on-the-job exchanges.
